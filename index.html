Nine Iterations

Follow the rhythm

Treat People with Kindness by Harry Styles is a retro rock song from a retro rock/ pop inspired album. The songs meaning is that of the title, he expresses how hopefully one day we can live in a world were we everyone can treat eachother with kindness. This song had so much power that when it came out that it inspired fans to create a TPWK Project, were they recorded random acts of kindness all over the world! These lyrics struck a chord with me and I felt they need to be heard and remembered, espcially needed with the year the world has had. I decided to go with a retro 70's style for the design as these lyrics radiate positivity and good vibes, much like that particualr era in time.

About me

Hi, I'm Caoimhe! I am a first year student at Ulster University studying Interaction Design and I hope to learn a lot within this course. I am passionate about art and design and I have many goals, one of my goals is to learn everything there is to know about user experience and designing for interfaces.This will guide me on my way to create beautiful and meaningful creations in the future. My manifesto is about self growth and I feel passionate about growing as a person and a designer and I know that this course will offer me copious amounts of chances to do so.

I am excited for the opportunites that this course could present to me. In the future I hope to design something that could possibly make a difference and help people and the world become a better place! The possibilities are endless and I cant wait to see what lies ahead...
